## Description: <br>
Local-first TikTok Growth OS for strategy, hooks, scripts, retention design, and analytics feedback. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External creators and content teams use this skill to plan TikTok positioning, content pillars, hooks, short-form scripts, captions, and retention-focused storyboards. They can also save local drafts and manually entered performance metrics for pattern review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Profile strategy, drafts, notes, captions, hooks, and manually entered performance metrics can be stored in local plaintext files. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid saving sensitive information and periodically review or delete files in ~/.openclaw/workspace/memory/tiktok/. <br>
Risk: Growth guidance and pattern reports may be mistaken for guaranteed audience or follower outcomes. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as strategic drafts, review before posting, and avoid presenting recommendations as guaranteed virality or growth. <br>
